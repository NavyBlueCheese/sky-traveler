## About

**Sky Traveler** is a single-page personality quiz that answers what kind of *sky traveler*
you are, represented by your very own hot-air balloon type and color. The whole experience
unfolds against a persistent, gently animated sky filled with drifting clouds, hot-air
balloons, paper planes, kites, glowing sky lanterns, and flocks of birds. Visual assets and narration were partially made with Claude assistance.
